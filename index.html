Got Questions?

We've answered the most frequently asked questions below. However, each product landing page has more specific information that you can explore.

Can I see the app in action before purchasing?

We provide screenshots and a full video run-through of the app which we make available at Envato where we sell the app exclusively. You will need Xcode to build iOS and macOS apps locally, and Android Studio to build the Android version. This can be done remotely using a service like CodeMagic if you don't have a Mac.

What are the requirements for using Startcommerce apps?

The lastest stable versions of Dart and Flutter installed, and knowledge of how to run and build Flutter apps is a minimum requirement. We suggest using FVM and provide guides on how to customize the app for your client or audience.

What is the difference between the regular and premium versions?

  • Regular versions of our apps come with a mobile and web app for the customer and desktop, mobile and web apps for a single-tenant business.

  • Premium versions also come with a platform app for multi-tenancy, and additional functionality like active security monitoring, mobile offline caching and additional auth methods like OAuth and local device auth.

Do you have a free trial?

No we don't provide a free trial. You can visualize the entire app by viewing our screenshots and watching our walkthrough videos.

How do you handle updates?

When you purchase an app from Startcommerce you can request an invite to our private GitHub repositories for that app. We publish all updates to those repositories in labeled branches. You can subscribe to those updates.

We also provide an archive file to Code Canyon. Envato will notify you of updates at the email address you provide on your Envato account.

I have an issue with my app

We provide support via GitHub Issues on the main repository for the app you purchased. All issues are visible and can be tracked by all members. We prioritize support in accordance with urgency of the issue as assessed by our team members, but you can rest assured we will provide an initial response within 24 hours.

What architecture do you subscribe to?

We use an architecture espoused by Robert Martin (Uncle Bob) called Clean Architecture.
